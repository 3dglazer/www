Welcome.

My name is Zdenek Glazer and this is my graphic portfolio. I am a 3D generalist, which means that I am able to design, model, animate, render and deliver 3D animation all alone, because I know all the aspects of it's creation. I love to work in a team though. Because it's a long way from a script to finished piece and both more fun and more productive.

Learn more about me or get in touch if you want to work with me on your next project.
